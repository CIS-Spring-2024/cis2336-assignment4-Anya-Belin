Welcome

The University of Houston is proud to open its sugarland location's first dining hall! Sugar Boulevard dining hall is thrilled to serve our cougars. A mouthwatering menu has been created to set this Sugarland dinning hall apart from other dining places on our other campuses. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or looking for a place to hang out in between classes, our vibrant atmosphere promises an amazing dining experience.

About Sugar Boulevard dining hall

Step into our inviting dining hall and embark on a culinary journey like no other. From savory street foods of Asia to the comforting classics of home-cooked Southern meals, our expansive menu reflects the diverse backgrounds and tastes of our student body. However, it's not just about the food here at Sugar Boulevard we want our students to have an amazing experince when dining. Our vibrant colors paired with modern architecture makes the space visually appealing. The dining hall provides seating for over 1,500 students and outlets for our technology students to charge up. Each day the menu brings the 5 different food stations with a new cuisine changing for every lunch period.

Our Suppliers

At Sugar Boulevard Dining Hall we want to provide our students with top of the line ingredients. Our commitment to quality starts with our suppliers as we take pride in partnering with local producers. Our partnerships with local farms ensure that our produce is always seasonal, ripe, and bursting with flavor, while our relationships with regional ranchers guarantee that our meats are humanely raised, antibiotic-free, and of the highest quality. With the help of Sysco we are able to transport these items. By choosing this dining you're not just enjoying a delicious meal you're supporting a network of passionate farmers, producers, and artisans who care about the food they grow and the communities they serve. So come dine with us and taste the difference that quality, locally sourced ingredients can make!
