Our Owner

Meet Carmen Boulevard Berzatto, the heart and soul behind Sugar Boulevard dining hall! With a passion for culinary excellence and a global perspective on flavors, Carmen brings a wealth of experience to our kitchen as both our proud owner and head chef. Carmen's journey in the culinary world began at the esteemed University of Houston culinary school, where he honed his skills and developed a deep appreciation for the art of cooking. Carmen embarked on a culinary adventure that took him across the globe, from bustling kitchens in Europe to exotic markets in Asia. Along the way, he immersed himself in diverse cuisines, learning traditional techniques and innovative approaches to shape his culinary identity. Despite his international success, Carmen's heart always remained in Houston. Driven by a desire to give back to his community and share his passion for food with our students, Carmen returned home to create something truly special Sugar Boulevard dining hall. As both owner and head chef, Carmen leads by example, inspiring his team to strive for excellence in every dish they create. With his guidance and mentorship, our staff is empowered to push the boundaries of creativity, constantly innovating and refining their craft to deliver unparalleled dining experiences for our students.
